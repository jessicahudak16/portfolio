G-Code

Overview

In my advanced SolidWorks course, I used SolidWorks CAM for simulating CNC machining processes and generating G-Code. For example, I automated the carving of my initials onto a wooden block.

Steps

1. I exported the G-Code from SolidWorks.

G17 G20 G40 G80 G90 T1
						G00 X0.0 Y0.0 Z0.5
						M03
						G04 P0.5
						
						G01 X0.2 Y1.2 F20.0
						G01 Z-0.1 F8.0
						G03 X0.8 I0.3 F15.0
						G01 Y2.1 
						G01 Z0.5 F8.0
						
						G91
						G01 X0.4 F20.0
						G01 Z-0.6 F8.0
						G01 Y-1.2 F15.0
						G01 X0.6 
						G01 Z0.6 F8.0
						
						G90
						G01 X2.2 F20.0
						G01 Z-0.1 F8.0
						G01 Y2.1 F15.0
						G01 Z0.5 F8.0
						G01 X2.8 F20.0
						G01 Z-0.1 F8.0
						G01 Y0.9 F15.0
						G01 Z0.5 F8.0
						G01 X2.2 Y1.5 F20.0
						G01 Z-0.1 F8.0
						G01 X2.8 F15.0
						G01 Z1.0 F8.0
						
						M05

2. I verified the preview using a free online tool.

3. I imported the G-Code to a CNC machine so that it carved out my initials on a wooden block.
