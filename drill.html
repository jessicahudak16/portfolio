Hand Drill

Overview

I collaborated with a team to reverse-engineer a Craftsman hand drill. We took apart the drill, assigned each member different parts to measure and model from scratch, and then assembled all our CAD models together.

You can view the final SolidWorks animation (comparing the real drill to our CAD model) at https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0Jf9nNhK9ns.
